Our Missions

The municipality's Missions is to provide financial aid to Aurora's college students. They will be assisted in achieving their future aspirations through this program.


Tuition and other school fees

all reasonable expenses including his studies, researches, matriculation, tuition, laboratory and other miscellaneous school fees.


Book allowance

Book allowance for education related book and supply expenses as determined by an institution for purposes of calculating a student 's financial.


MS/PE Uniform

MS/PE Uniform is a kind of allowance given to the Students who are ordered to wear a prescribed dress at the time of their schools


Transportation allowance

Transportation allowance means for the reasonable, actual costs of moving unprocessed gas, residue gas, or gas plant products to a point of sale.


Monthly living allowance

The monthly living allowance is paid from the month of arrival of the student and is further transferred at the beginning of each month.


Graduation clothing allowance

Graduation Clothing allowances may be issued to help student pay for their uniforms to be able to attend to their dream Graduation.
